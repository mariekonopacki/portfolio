About
Marie Konopacki
Even at 2 years old, I had an eye for design...

I'm a senior at Harvard University studying Art, Film, & Visual Studies, with a secondary in Computer Science. As an artist, coder, and designer, I'm interested in how people interact with what they use every day, and I seek to foster inclusion through design.

Throughout my life, I've witnessed most of my family—none of whom have gone to college and most of whom do not speak English—struggle with using things not designed for them, which has led to my desire to focus on accessibility and diversity when creating.
